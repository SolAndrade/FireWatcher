How can you report a local fire?

Reporting a local fire is a critical and time-sensitive matter and there are some ways to report it:

  • Go to the right menu and you'll see your current location. You may want to indicate observations of the fire and submit your report in case you consider it useful for the authorities.
  • Call the emergency services number for fires, medical emergencies, and other urgent situations if you believe there is a fire that needs attention. You will have to provide your locationand a detailed description for responders to find and act quickly.
