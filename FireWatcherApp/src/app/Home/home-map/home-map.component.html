Why does the risk of fire increase?

One of the causes of the high risk of fires in Spain is the high temperatures. A new heat wave is expected during these days. Thermometers are around 40º C in some parts of Spain which, together with the drought and lack of precipitation, increases the probability of fires. Several provinces are under heat warnings this Tuesday at risk due to high temperatures and they could reach up to 42ºC, according to the prediction of the State Meteorological Agency (AEMET).

Measures to extend fire prevention and extinction throughout the year

The Council of Ministers has approved a Royal Decree with urgent measures to improve the prevention and extinction of forest fires throughout the year. Minister Teresa Ribera assures that the territories must have annual planning.
